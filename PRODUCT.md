# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Motivated tech professionals actively job-hunting — engineers, developers, technical PMs. They arrive with a goal and a deadline, carrying background stress but moving with intent. They are capable, impatient with friction, and will notice quality details.

## Product Purpose

CV Tech Tailor gives job seekers an AI-powered workflow to tailor their resume to a specific job description: import their profile, paste a JD, review a highlighted diff, and export an ATS-ready document. The AI enhances and rewords — it never fabricates. Every change requires explicit human approval before export.

## Brand Personality

Precise, confident, warm. This is a professional tool that respects the user's intelligence without feeling cold. Not a cheerleader, not a bureaucrat — a skilled collaborator.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS look: cream backgrounds, pastel accents, rounded-everything, Notion-clone aesthetics
- Loveable/cute: over-friendly, Duolingo-style, excessive illustration or emoji-driven UI
- Job board aesthetics: LinkedIn, Indeed — corporate, dense, cluttered
- Gradient text, glassmorphism, hero-metric templates

## Design Principles

1. **Confidence through clarity** — the interface respects user intelligence; no hand-holding copy, no redundant labels
2. **Warmth without whimsy** — professional surfaces with considered softness; never cold, never cute
3. **The work is the hero** — the resume, the diff, the gap report are what matter; chrome stays quiet
4. **Portfolio-grade craft** — every detail deliberate; this is a demonstration of what good UI looks like
5. **Calm momentum** — motivated users need flow, not friction; every screen moves them one step forward

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Keyboard navigable. No motion that can't be reduced.
